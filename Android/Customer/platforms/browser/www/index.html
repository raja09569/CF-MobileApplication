About CamerounFacile

Launched in June 2017, camerounfacile.com is today ranked as the leading provider of consumer-direct travel services in Cameroun. CamerounFacile.com has emerged as he most trusted travel brand in Cameroun, and has own three awards at the Cameroun tourism awards felicitated for 'Outstanding performance as a domestic tour operator(Rest of Cameroun)', 'Outstanding performance as a domestic tour operator in Douala and Yaounde' and 'Outstanding performance as an Inbound to operator-CatC' and Amongst many others over the years. Positioned as a brand that believes in " Creating Happy Travellers", CamerounFacile.com provides information,Pricing, Availability, And Booking Facility for domestic and Cabs, Bus Bookings and Posting AD. We offer a host of travel services designed to make business and leisure travel easier.
